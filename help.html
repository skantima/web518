Help? Here it is

  • Setting up your account

    • Instructions

    1. New users can sign up by clicking on the Signup link on the login page. The image is shown below.
    2. Fill out the required fields and click 'SignIn'.

  • Login to your account

    • Instructions

    1. Once you Signup, you can login to your account with your User name and Password on the login page. The image is shown below.
    2. Fill out the required fields and click 'Login'.

  • Changing Your Profile Picture

    • Instructions

    1. Once the User is logged in, at the top right corner, you will find "Account" where you can find your personal information.
    2. Please click on the 'Choose File' option right below it.
    3. Please choose a .jpg or a .png file.
    4. Click on 'Upload' after having chosen a file.

  • Logout

    • Instructions

    1. Users can logout by clicking on Logout button.
    2. The 'Logout' is at the right top of the screen as shown in the image below.

Groups

  • Group Memberships

    • Instructions

    1. Users will be a part of Global group in this website. Users can be part of different groups based on their interests

  • Creating a new group or adding Members to the group

    • Instructions

    1. Users can create a new group by clicking on the drop down under Groups in the left corner of the home page to create a new group or to invite a member into a group as shown in the figure below.
    2. Provide a name for your group, its purpose and set its visibility to the users by choosinyou'd like to have on your Group.
    3. Users have options to create either a public Group in which any user could join or a private Group which requires an invite from the member of the Group.
    4. A user will have to remain logged-in for creating a Group.

  • Invite members

    • Instructions

    1. The member of a Group can invite other users to the Group that has been created.
    2. A user can select from the list of public groups that are displayed. Even the private channels will be displayed 'if' they have been created by that user.
    3. A group member can invite new members

  • User Search

    • Instructions

    1. Users can look up other users in the search bar and view their profile, located on the top left side of the webpage.

  • Post

    • Instructions

    1. Users can post about the items they are looking for or if they wanted to sell something. Refer the image below.
    2. Users can also upload local images, post code snippets, and upload image urls by clicking on the insert link option as shown in the image above.
    3. Image urls can be of the formats - .png, .jpeg/jpg, .gif, .tiff/tif.
    4. Please note that empty messages cannot be posted.
    5. Please refrain from using profane or vulgar language.

  • Reactions

    • Instructions

    1. Users can also reply to the existing posts. Replies could be in the form of a message or a code.
    2. Users can react to already existing posts.
    3. Users can either like or dislike a post.
